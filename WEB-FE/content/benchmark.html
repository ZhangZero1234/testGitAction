Open standard benchmarking assessments

IBM participates in an internationally recognized open standard benchmarking partnership with APQC, a not-for-profit research organization. The benchmarking content is built upon a standard cross-country Process Classification Framework, enabling business performance to be compared across organizations and industries.

Visit the APQC website http://www.apqc.org/
