How to order printed copies of IBV content:

  1. Access the IBM Print on Demand system for GBS

  2. Make a note of the document ID number that you require (i.e. 58018158USEN or GBE03023). To ensure that you get the latest version and are able to see any translations, do not include the document suffix (-01, -02 etc.). You can find the document number on the last page of the PDF report in the lower right corner.

  3. Enter the document ID number into the "Search by Document ID" box.
  4. Click on the document ID to see the document details page.
  5. Order prints by placing the number of copies needed into your shopping cart. You may need to scroll down to see this field and the action buttons.
  6. Click on "Checkout" or "Continue Shopping" as appropriate. Once you have finished ordering documents for printing, click on "Checkout" again.
  7. After you click "Checkout," you will be required to provide information, including your name, email, shipping address, required delivery date, and billing details. Cost is recovered against the account codes provided for each order. Extra charges apply for expedited shipping.
  8. Submit the order. You will be given an order reference number that can be used for queries.
